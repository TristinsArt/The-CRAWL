**Level:** 2nd-level
**Casting Time:** 1 action
**Range:** 15 feet
**Components:** V, S, M (a deck of cards)
**Duration:** Instantaneous
**School:** [[Spells/Schools of Magic/Conjuration\|Conjuration]]

You spray a 15-foot cone of spectral cards. Each creature in that area must make a Dexterity saving throw. On a failed save, a creature takes **2d10** force damage and has the [[Rules/References/Conditions/Blinded\|blinded]] condition until the end of its next turn. On a successful save, a creature takes half as much damage only.
